Benjamin Ahlbrand

I'm working with Ken Perlin as a Research Scientist at the Future Reality Lab at NYU, where I explore the intersection between procedural generation, machine learning and computer graphics. I enjoy new diverse, challenges that open my eyes to new possibilities. I have a broad set of interests, while focusing on computer graphics, and leveraging machine learning as a powerful tool in my research towards democratizing Future Reality.

Check out my blog to learn more about my work.

I am a co-founder and the CTO of Autotoon where I drive development of an experimental low latency real-time framework for social AR / VR - in addition to bringing procedural animation and machine learning together to breathe life into shared worlds with virtual characters.

I recently completed my Masters in Computer Science (MSCS) at New York University - Courant Institute of Mathematics, focusing on machine learning and computer graphics. I was a Teaching Assistant for Introduction to Programming, holding office hours and evaluating / providing feedback on weekly assignments.

During my undergrad I studied Computer Science specializing in Computer Graphics & Visualization and Machine Intelligence (B.S. in CS) at Purdue University (West Lafayette, Indiana), I was a Research Assistant and later a Research Fellow at VACCINE where my work resulted in several publications, and a Teaching Assistant for Software Engineering in the CS department (CS307). I guided and evaluated several teams of 4-6 students each semester in developing open ended semester-long projects utilizing Agile Scrum methodologies.
